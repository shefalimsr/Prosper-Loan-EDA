Loan Data from Prosper Data Exploration¶

by Shefali Mishra¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the loan amount taken by the different categories of borrowers. Also, the variation the Delinquent and Good borrowers had in various categories like BorrowerAPR , credit score etc.

Dataset Overview¶

This data set contains 113,937 loans with 81 variables on each loan, including loan amount, interest rate , current loan status, borrower income,employment status and credit history etc .

These three are negatively correlated to ProsperScore , but again there is no strong correlation.

I wanted to look at Chargedoff, Completed, Current and Defaulted LoanStatus. Chargedoff Loans have high ProsperScore , but they were not prominent after 2013, Same is the case for Defaulted LoanStatus. Also, it increased for Completed LoanStatus , perhaps reflecting the new strategy of Investors to select loans from the middle ProsperScore distribution.
